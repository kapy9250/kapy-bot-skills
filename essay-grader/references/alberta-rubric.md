# Alberta Personal Response to Texts Rubric

Alberta Education标准，适用于English Language Arts 10-1/20-1/30-1的Personal Response作文。

---

## 评分概述

Alberta采用**双维度**评分：
- **Ideas and Impressions**: 0-5分
- **Presentation**: 0-5分
- **总分**: 10分

### 与50分制的换算

| Alberta总分 (10) | 50分制等效 | 字母等级 |
|------------------|-----------|----------|
| 9-10 | 45-50 | A |
| 8 | 40-44 | B+ |
| 7 | 37-39 | B/B- |
| 6 | 33-36 | C+ |
| 5 | 30-32 | C |
| 4 | 25-29 | C-/D+ |
| 3 | 20-24 | D |
| 2 | 15-19 | D- |
| 0-1 | Below 15 | F |

## Dimension A: Ideas and Impressions (0-5)

评估学生对prompting text的**探索质量**、**想法与印象**、以及**支撑论述**。

### 5 - Excellent

> The student's exploration of the topic is **purposeful**. Perceptions and/or ideas are **thoughtful**. Support is **specific** and **relevantly reinforces** the student's ideas and impressions.

**特征:**
- 对话题的探索有明确目的和方向
- 想法和感受经过深思熟虑，不是表面反应
- 引用/举例精准，直接支撑自己的观点
- 能从prompting text出发，延伸到更深层的个人理解

**示例表现:**
- "The image of the empty chair reminded me of how we preserve absence—my grandmother's seat at dinner remains hers even now..."
- 将文本与个人经历建立有意义的联系

### 4 - Proficient

> The student's exploration of the topic is **revealing**. Perceptions and/or ideas are **meaningful and sensible**. Support is **appropriate** and **strengthens** the student's ideas and impressions.

**特征:**
- 探索过程有所发现，揭示了一些洞见
- 想法合理且有意义
- 支撑材料恰当，能强化论点
- 展现了对文本的genuine engagement

**与Excellent的区别:**
- 洞见是"sensible"而非"thoughtful"——合理但未必深刻
- 支撑是"appropriate"而非"specific"——恰当但不够精准

### 3 - Satisfactory

> The student's exploration of the topic is **generalized**. Perceptions and/or ideas are **straightforward and appropriate**. Support is **adequate** and **clarifies** the student's ideas and impressions.

**特征:**
- 探索停留在概括层面，缺少具体深入
- 想法直接、恰当，但缺乏独特性
- 支撑足够说明问题，但不突出
- 完成了任务要求，但没有超越

**常见问题:**
- 观点是"对的"但everyone would say this
- 引用存在但像是因为"应该引用"
- 缺少"so what"——为什么这对我重要

### 2 - Limited

> The student's exploration of the topic is **vague**. Perceptions and/or ideas are **superficial and/or ambiguous**. Support is **imprecise** and/or **ineffectively related** to the student's ideas and impressions.

**特征:**
- 探索方向模糊，不知道想说什么
- 想法浮于表面或含糊不清
- 支撑材料不精确，或与观点关联不紧
- 读者需要猜测作者的意图

**典型问题:**
- "This poem made me think about life"——什么关于life？
- 引用存在但不清楚为什么选这段
- 个人经历与文本的联系牵强

### 1 - Poor

> The student's exploration of the topic is **minimal**. Perceptions and/or ideas are **underdeveloped and/or irrelevant**. Support is **lacking** and/or **unrelated** to the student's ideas and impressions.

**特征:**
- 几乎没有真正的探索
- 想法未展开或与话题无关
- 缺少支撑或支撑与观点无关
- 可能严重偏题

### 0 - Insufficient

> The student has written so little that it is not possible to assess Ideas and Impressions OR the marker can discern no evidence of an attempt to address the task presented in the assignment.

**适用情况:**
- 字数极少无法评估
- 完全没有尝试回应assignment要求
- 提交空白或无关内容

## Dimension B: Presentation (0-5)

评估**voice**、**stylistic choices**（语言质量与正确性）、**tone**、以及**unifying/aesthetic effect**。

**重要原则:** 
> Consider the proportion of error in terms of the complexity and length of the response.

错误容忍度与文章复杂度和长度成正比。

### 5 - Excellent

> The voice created by the student is **distinct**. Stylistic choices are **specific** and student's creation of tone is **competent**. The unifying effect and/or aesthetic effect is **capably developed**.

**特征:**
- Voice独特鲜明，读者能感受到"这个人"
- 文体选择是specific的——有意识的决定，而非随意
- 语气控制得当
- 全文有统一的美学效果或情感基调

**示例:**
- 文章从头到尾保持一种沉思的、略带忧伤的tone
- 用词、句式、节奏都服务于这个整体效果
- 读起来像"一件作品"而非"完成作业"

### 4 - Proficient

> The voice created by the student is **apparent** and, at times, **distinct**. Stylistic choices and the student's creation of tone are **considered and occasionally effective**. The unifying effect and/or aesthetic effect is **appropriately developed**.

**特征:**
- Voice明显，偶尔突出
- 文体选择经过考虑，有时效果好
- 语气创造是intentional的
- 有统一效果，发展恰当

**与Excellent的区别:**
- Voice "at times distinct"——不是始终如一
- Stylistic choices "occasionally effective"——有成功也有平淡
- 整体效果是"appropriate"而非"capable"

### 3 - Satisfactory

> The voice created by the student is **apparent**. Stylistic choices are **adequate** and the student's creation of tone is **conventional**. The unifying effect and/or aesthetic effect is **adequately developed**.

**特征:**
- Voice存在但不突出
- 文体选择足够但常规
- 语气是conventional的——正确但无特色
- 统一效果adequate——及格水平

**常见问题:**
- 读起来像"学生作文"
- 语言正确但generic
- 没有明显错误，也没有亮点

### 2 - Limited

> The voice created by the student is **indistinct**. Stylistic choices are **imprecise** and the student's creation of tone is **inconsistent**. The unifying effect and/or aesthetic effect is **inadequately developed**.

**特征:**
- Voice模糊，感受不到作者
- 文体选择不精确
- 语气不一致——时正式时随意
- 缺乏统一感

**典型问题:**
- 文章前半部分是一种风格，后半变了
- 用词忽高忽低，academic混着slang
- 读者不确定这是什么类型的文章

### 1 - Poor

> The voice created by the student is **obscure**. Stylistic choices **impede communication** and the student's creation of tone is **ineffective**. A unifying effect and/or aesthetic effect is **absent**.

**特征:**
- Voice难以辨认
- 文体选择妨碍了沟通
- 语气无效
- 没有统一或美学效果

**严重问题:**
- 语言问题导致读者confusion
- 不知道作者想表达什么情感
- 文章各部分像是不同人写的

### 0 - Insufficient

> 与Ideas and Impressions相同标准——内容太少无法评估。

## Personal Response的文体特点

与Argumentative/Analytical Essay不同，Personal Response:

### 允许且鼓励:
- 使用第一人称 "I"
- 分享个人经历和情感
- 主观印象和感受
- 与prompting text的个人联系
- 创造性的文体选择

### 仍然需要:
- 与prompting text的明确联系
- 支撑和发展想法（不是纯感想流水账）
- 清晰的表达
- 语言的基本正确性

### 评估时的调整:
- 不要求formal academic thesis
- "I think/feel"在此文体中是appropriate的
- Voice的个人色彩是优点而非缺点
- 情感真实性是重要评价维度

## 错误容忍度指南

Alberta明确要求考虑错误与篇幅/复杂度的比例：

| 文章长度 | 复杂度 | 容忍度 |
|----------|--------|--------|
| 500字以下 | 简单句式 | 低——少量错误即显著 |
| 500-800字 | 中等句式 | 中——允许若干minor errors |
| 800字以上 | 复杂句式 | 高——关注pattern而非individual errors |

**评估原则:**
- 长文章中的isolated errors不应严重扣分
- 关注是否有**pattern of errors**（系统性问题）
- 复杂句式的尝试应被肯定，即使偶有失误
- 短文章中的错误更显眼，但也要考虑学生是否在"play it safe"

## 与50分制Rubric的对应

使用Alberta标准时，可参照以下映射：

| Alberta维度 | 50分制对应维度 |
|-------------|---------------|
| Ideas and Impressions | Content & Ideas (部分) + Advanced Skills (部分) |
| Presentation - Voice | Language & Style - Voice & Tone |
| Presentation - Stylistic Choices | Language & Style - Diction & Sentence Variety |
| Presentation - Tone | Language & Style - Voice & Tone |
| Presentation - Unifying Effect | Organization (部分) + Advanced Skills - Genuine Engagement |
| (隐含) Conventions | Conventions |

**注意:** Alberta的Presentation已包含部分conventions评估（"quality and correctness of language"），但不单独列出。如需详细追踪语法问题，仍参照50分制的Conventions维度。
